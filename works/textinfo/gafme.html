Going Away From Me
  • Writer: Param Siddharth
  • Type: Poem
  • Language: English
  • Year: 2018
; Far somewhere, In the silently loud night, My soul is fading Into eternity. I beckon her to stay, But she goes farther. The more I plead, The lesser of her I see. As time passes, My love for her grows, Whence I shall cherish All my moments with her. My love shall thrive Till my very end.
